Nigeria's Road Safety Crisis

The Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC) recorded over 13,000 road traffic crashes and more than 5,000 fatalities in a single year. The majority are preventable — occurring under predictable conditions: heavy rainfall, peak-hour congestion, high-speed corridors. Fixed-cycle signals worsen the problem by creating "dead time" — green lights for empty lanes while congested lanes wait. This system addresses that gap.
